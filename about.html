About JKBOSE

Introduction

The Jammu and Kashmir Board of School Education (JKBOSE) was established in 1975 under the Jammu and Kashmir Board of School Education Act, 1975. It is the main board for school education in the Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir.

JKBOSE is responsible for prescribing courses of instruction, textbooks, and conducting examinations for school education in the region. The board ensures quality education and fair examination practices for all affiliated schools.

Our Vision

To provide quality education that empowers students to become responsible citizens and contribute to the development of society. We aim to create an inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth.

Our Mission

To develop and implement a robust education system that meets national standards while addressing regional needs. We strive to conduct fair examinations, update curriculum regularly, and support schools in delivering quality education.

Key Functions

  • Prescribing courses of instruction and textbooks for school education
  • Conducting examinations for Secondary and Higher Secondary levels
  • Granting recognition to schools
  • Providing academic support to affiliated institutions
  • Developing and updating curriculum as per national standards
  • Publishing results and issuing certificates
